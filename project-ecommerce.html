eCommerce websites

Designed various eCommerce websites such as antimalware software, VOD streaming platforms, fitness videos, IQ games and more.

Company: intellForce
Role: UX and UI Design team leader

Overview

These are some B2C eCommerce projects that offer different services for users. As the lead designer for these projects, I was in charge of overseeing the whole user experience, art direction and front-end development.
Each project comprised UX work, including planning the information architecture, wireframing and testing, and UI work, including art direction, branding, and composing a design language.

Master Protection PC Privacy

Anti-malware software UX|UI & branding

Master Protection is a B2C anti-malware software for Windows PCs. It helps protect privacy from online threats. As a B2C product, this software is used by a variety of users and personas. The UX and UI design needs to be very approachable and user-friendly, as it was meant to be used by not-so-tech-savvy users.

I worked on the design of the website and the dashboard from which the users downloaded the software and the PC scanning software itself. I also worked on the branding and designed the logo.
