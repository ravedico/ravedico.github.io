Case study:
ClixScale

Marketing campaigns monitoring tool

Company: ClixScale by IntellForce

Role: Product Designer

Timeline: 5 months

Deliverables: User research, wireframes, prototypes, UI design and design system creation

Teammates: Product Managers, fullstack developers

Overview

ClixScale is a web application for marketing campaign managers and financial teams to control and monitor marketing campaigns and bank accounts. This is an interesting product that was born out of a need of the company for a unique customizable platform for monitoring marketing activity. The uniqueness of this system on the competitor's products in the market is in its simplicity.

The challenge

Marketing teams struggle with fragmented communication and complex campaign management processes. Existing solutions lack:

  • Real-time performance tracking
  • Easy campaign selection
  • Clear communication channels
  • Transparent reporting mechanisms

Research Process

Surveys

We composed a series of questions, sent it to partners and shared it within marketing communities.

Customer Interviews

We conducted several interviews with key partners about their current communication with publishers and the way they run campaigns and heard their thoughts about missing features they have in the current tools.

User Research
  • Created personas for campaign managers and affiliates
  • Mapped user journey and interaction touchpoints

Key Research Findings

  • Difficulty tracking multi-channel campaign performance
  • Complex affiliate onboarding processes
  • Limited visibility into campaign metrics
  • Inefficient communication workflows

    • Design solution

  • Interface including campaigns list and their data
  • Performance dashboard with real-time stats
  • Responsive design for all devices
    • Design Process

    Information Architecture
