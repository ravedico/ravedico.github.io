ClixScale

B2B dashboard and website
The project

ClixScale is a Saas platform for marketing and financial teams to control and monitor marketing campaigns and bank accounts.

My role

I had designed the brand graphic language, built it's website and designed the platform that the company's marketing team use to monitor their campaign and financial activity.

  • Wireframing and designing the platform for the marketing and finance team.
  • Branding and creating a graphic language.
  • Designing and building the company's website.
  • Visit website
Campaigns dashboard

I worked on a Saas product for marketing teams and affiliates. Here the users view and monitor online campaigns activity, create campaigns and review performance.

View project in Figma

Bank activity reports dashboard

I worked on a platform that uses the financial team. Here they can view and monitor the company's financial activity. Each box represents a bank account.
The challenge here was to make it visually prominent, so the users will have the critical information at first glance, so they can make important decisions fast.
The design process was composed from working on an individual info box, and on how the general UX layout of the page. The criteria was to show as many info boxes as possible in the above the line screen section.
