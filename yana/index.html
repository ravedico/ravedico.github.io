My story

I’m a multidisciplinary creative and digital artist with a global mindset. I have vast experience across motion design, 3d modelling, compositing, video editing, post production, styling and music.
My passion lies in creating art of many forms. Throughout my diverse career, I have worked with companies in various industries including video art studios, gaming companies and fashion studios.
